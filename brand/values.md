# Mission, Story, and Values

## Mission

> The Trainer Page is more than a gym. We are on a mission to show members that they can do more than what their minds allow them to believe. One member at a time we will motivate them, break limiting beliefs, restore hope, and give 110% of our commitment to improve both their physical and mental health. In Sweat Sessions, we use fitness as a tool to remind us of not only our physical strength, but also our mental strength.

## Our Story

The Trainer Page began its journey in **2018**, initially as a personal training company. While we offered group training, it wasn't until the period after COVID that we truly found our footing. In **November 2020**, The Trainer Page evolved into the fitness studio it is today — marked by our dedication to fostering a supportive community through Sweat Sessions.

## Our Why

Our founder was inspired by a personal journey through fitness, discovering the potent role of exercise in managing anxiety and depression. This personal journey ignited a passion not just for fitness but for the business of empowering others.

## Core Values

### 1. Obsessive Attention to Detail (OAD)
Practicing excellence and meticulousness in every session, every interaction, every rep.

### 2. Dedicated to the Sweat
Embracing humility, loyalty, consistency, and a growth mindset. The work is the point.

### 3. Extreme Ownership
Taking initiative and responsibility for collective success. No excuses, no finger-pointing.

### 4. Put in Work (PIW)
Committing to the rigorous work required for excellence. No shortcuts.

### 5. Have Fun
Ensuring enjoyment and optimism are at the heart of everything we do.
